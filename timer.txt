Timer Frequencies in GuildWorkOrders
=====================================

HEARTBEAT SYSTEM
----------------
- 3 seconds: Main heartbeat timer (modules/Sync.lua:931)
  - Rotates through orders, sending 1 order per heartbeat
  - Initial heartbeat delay: 5 seconds after startup

CLEANUP TIMERS
--------------
- 30 seconds: Two periodic cleanup timers (GuildWorkOrders.lua:41 & 48)
  1. Order expiration cleanup - expires active orders after 1 minute TTL
  2. Order purging cleanup - transitions non-active orders through lifecycle states

ORDER LIFECYCLE TIMINGS
-----------------------
- 1 minute: Active orders expire (become EXPIRED status)
- 2 minutes: Non-active orders (expired/cancelled/completed/cleared) transition to PURGED
- 4 minutes: PURGED orders are deleted (after 2 min broadcast period)

OTHER TIMERS
------------
- 0.2 seconds: Broadcast queue processing timer (modules/Database.lua:556)
- 3 seconds: UI notification fade delay (modules/UI.lua:1536)
- 5 seconds: UI notification fade delay for errors (modules/UI.lua:1564)

MESSAGE QUEUE
-------------
- MESSAGE_DELAY: Variable delay for queue processing (modules/Sync.lua:161)
